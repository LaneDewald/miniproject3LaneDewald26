About This App

This is a simple movie watchlist app built with Flask for INF601 Advanced Programming with Python.

You can:

  • Create an account and log in
  • Add movies to your personal watchlist
  • Mark movies as watched or unwatched
  • Remove movies from your list

Built by Lane DeWald using Flask, SQLite, and Bootstrap 5.
